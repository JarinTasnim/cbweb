FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

Definition:

1. What is CashBaba?

CashBaba is an e-Wallet service of Recursion FinTech Limited. Recursion FinTech Limited is the Licensed Payment Service Provider of Bangladesh with PCI-DSS and ISO-27001 Compliance Certification along with EMVCo Standard. It is committed to offer you a cash-less, worry-less, and secure platform that takes care of your everyday financial transaction needs! This versatile payment solution allows an individual or a merchant to do financial transactions without cash or a card.

2. What is PCI DSS?

Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard. PCI DSS certification ensures the highest level of security for the payment industry. The PCI Security Standards Council (PCI SSC) is a global forum that brings together payments industry stakeholders to develop and drive the adoption of data security standards and resources for safe payments worldwide.
