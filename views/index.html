About this awesome app

CashBaba is an e-Wallet and Online Payment Gateway service of Recursion FinTech Limited. Recursion FinTech Limited is the Licensed Payment Service Provider of Bangladesh with PCI-DSS and ISO-27001 Compliance Certification along with EMVCo Standard. It is committed towards offering you with a cash-less, worry-less and a secure platform that takes care of your everyday financial transaction needs! This versatile payment solution allows an individual or a merchant to do financial transactions without cash or card. It empowers is a users to make financial transactions in real time with the help of their mobile phone from anywhere at any time. CashBaba will provide you a convenient payment platform at an affordable price along with the facilities of managing your accounts easily with a maximized level of security.

Payment service provider

We are licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Bangladesh as a Payment Service Provider (PSP) with accord to "Bangladesh Payment and Settlement Systems Regulation-2014 (BPSSR-2014)”

PCI verified payment

We are PCI-DSS Certified Payment Service Provider in Bangladesh. PCI DSS certification ensures the highest level of security for the payment industry.

ISO 27001 and ISO 9001 Certificate

With the goal of providing quality and consistent service, avoiding financial penalties and losses associated with data breaches, we are ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certified company.
