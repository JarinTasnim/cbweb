Mobile Recharge

Your prepaid or postpaid mobile recharge is just a click away with CashBaba! We provide Mobile Recharge services for all the mobile operators of Bangladesh including GrameenPhone, Robi, Airtel, Banglalink and TeleTalk. You can also recharge the mobile number of your friends and family with this app.

Add Money

Adding money to your CashBaba wallet is quick, easy and secure. It is necessary to add money to your CashBaba wallet or receive fund from another user before commencing any financial transaction through this application. You can add money to your CashBaba wallet from your bank account or your debit/credit card instantly. It will also enable you to add multiple bank accounts or cards to your wallet. You will be able to use this fund to make payments at various places such as departmental stores, restaurants, hospitals, educational institutions, mobile recharge platform, while online payments at any merchant’s place.

Send Money

CashBaba is the simplest, most efficient, and cost-free method of sending money to your friends and family. You can send money to anyone at anytime and anywhere with just a finger tap. We allow you to receive and send money at absolutely free of cost.

CashBaba Card

This is the first time in Bangladesh that a card network has crossed the barrier of forming a primary membership alliance with a fintech company other than a bank in providing its platform users a free card with every wallet in the country. This partnership with UnionPay International will enable you to enjoy the benefits of co-branded card facilities. By using this card you can access your balance at 61,000 POS machines around the country, most E-Commerce sites and withdraw cash from the 11,000 ATM machines across the country without interest or any annual fee.

Transfer

You can easily transfer your wallet balance at anytime simply, by clicking on “Transfer” on your application dashboard and choosing the amount of money you want to transfer.

QR Payment

CashBaba EMVCo standard QR code facilitates the worldwide interoperability and acceptance of secure payment transactions. You can now simply scan the QR code of CashBaba network from your CashBaba application and make the payment instantly. The payments made through QR code will be directly gets transferred to the merchant account.

Virtual Cash

Without providing cash or sharing your card data, you can help your friends and family to use your money at any merchant point or for online payment. Generate a virtual cash number for any specific amount or for a specific merchant and share this number with the beneficiary. They will then, use this number while making payment at the merchant point or e-commerce site after which the payment for that specific amount will be fulfilled.

Request Money

Requesting money has never been easier. Simply use the mobile number of your friends or family who has a CashBaba account and request for money. No cost when requesting money from friends and family, making it easier than ever to send money.
