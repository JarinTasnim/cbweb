The security and integrity of your personal information is our highest priority and we use the latest technology and security solutions to provide you the most secured payment system.

PCI verified payment

We are the only PCI-DSS Certified Payment Service Provider in Bangladesh. PCI DSS certification ensures the highest level of security for the payment industry.


ISO 27001 and ISO 9001 Certificate

With the goal of providing quality and consistent service, avoiding financial penalties and losses associated with data breaches and to protect and enhance our reputation complying with business, legal, contractual and regulatory requirements, we are ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certified company.


Vulnerability Assessment

Our certified and skilled IT professionals conduct in-depth internal vulnerability assessment on a monthly basis and mitigate or take preventive actions for all possible chances of vulnerabilities. We also perform external vulnerability assessment in every six months with the help of certified and professional third-party organization.


Penetration Testing

We also conduct a comprehensive range of Penetration Testing to ensure that our application is completely protected against all possible threats of penetration.


Encryption and Tokenization Technology

We use encryption and tokenization technology to protect all sensitive data of the user. This technique allows only valid and authorized request to access the data which ensures that even if it is in wrong hand it can't be accessed since it is not in a readable format. Only system itself can access the data and no one has access to it.


Two-Factor Authentication

To keep your transaction safe and secure as an additional layer of security, we have used two-factor authentication- User Name & Password and OTP. With this additional level of security, user's transactions become much safer. It safeguards sensitive information from any malpractices.
